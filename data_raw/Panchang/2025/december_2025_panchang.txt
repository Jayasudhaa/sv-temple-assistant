This panchang provides daily Hindu calendar information including:
- Tithi (lunar day)
- Nakshatra (lunar mansion/constellation)
- Temple events and special poojas
- Abhishekam schedule
- Festival dates

HOW TO USE:
- Times shown are in Denver Mountain Time
- "Tithi" shows the lunar day and its end time
- "Nakshatra" shows the constellation and its transition time
- "Event" shows temple observances and festivals for that day

SPECIAL DECEMBER FESTIVALS:

KARTHIKA DEEPAM (Dec 4):
Festival of lights celebrating Lord Shiva. Lighting lamps symbolizes dispelling darkness and ignorance.

DHANURMASAM (Dec 15 - Jan 14):
Most auspicious month for Lord Vishnu worship. Daily Suprabhatam seva, special archanas, and reading of Bhagavad Gita are performed.

SRI VAIKUNTA EKADASI (Dec 30):
The most important Ekadasi of the year. Believed that the gates of Vaikunta (Lord Vishnu's abode) are open on this day. Fasting and special poojas are performed.
