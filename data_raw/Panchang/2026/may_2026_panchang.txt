SPECIAL MAY FESTIVALS:

SRI KURMA JAYANTI (May 1):
Birthday of Lord Kurma (tortoise avatar of Vishnu). Second avatar that appeared during the churning of the ocean (Samudra Manthan) to support Mount Mandara.

SRI ANNAMACHARYA JAYANTI (May 1):
Birth anniversary of Sri Tallapaka Annamacharya (1408-1503), the great composer of devotional songs (sankirtanas) dedicated to Lord Venkateswara. Composed over 32,000 devotional compositions.

SRI HANUMAN JAYANTI (May 11):
Birthday of Lord Hanuman, the devoted servant of Lord Rama. Special celebrations with prayers, bhajans, and reading of Hanuman Chalisa. Fasting and special poojas observed.

SRI SANISCHARA JAYANTI (May 16):
Birth anniversary of Lord Shani (Saturn deity). Special prayers offered to alleviate Saturn-related difficulties. Occurs on Amavasya, making it doubly significant.

NAMMAZHWAR TIRUNAKSHATRAM (May 29):
Birth star of Nammazhwar (Sadagopan), one of the twelve Alvars and the greatest Vaishnava saint poet. Composed Thiruvaimozhi, considered the Tamil Veda.

ADHIKA MAASA (LEAP MONTH):
May 16 - June 13 is Adhika Jyeshta Maasa (extra/leap month added to align lunar and solar calendars). During Adhika Maasa:
- Regular festivals are generally not celebrated
- Auspicious ceremonies like weddings are avoided
- Good time for spiritual practices, meditation, charity
- This is why May has two Purnimas
