SPECIAL JANUARY FESTIVALS:

MAKARA SANKRANTI / PONGAL (Jan 14):
Harvest festival marking the sun's transition into Capricorn (Makara). Uttarayanam (northward journey of the sun) begins, considered highly auspicious. Pongal is the Tamil harvest festival celebrated with great fervor.

BHOGI (Jan 13):
Day before Pongal, associated with discarding old things and welcoming new beginnings. Sri Goda (Andal) Kalyanam is also celebrated.

KANUMA (Jan 15):
Third day of Pongal celebrations, dedicated to cattle and farm animals.

VASANTA PANCHAMI (Jan 23):
Marks the beginning of spring. Dedicated to Goddess Saraswati. Auspicious for starting education and learning.

RATHA SAPTAMI (Jan 25):
Birthday of Sun God (Surya). Symbolizes the sun's chariot driven by seven horses representing seven days of the week.

BHEESHMA EKADASI (Jan 28):
Associated with Bhishma Pitamaha from Mahabharata. Observing this Ekadasi is considered highly meritorious.
