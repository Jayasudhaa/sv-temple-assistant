SPECIAL DECEMBER FESTIVALS:

DHANURMASAM (Dec 16 - Jan 14, 2027):
Most sacred month for Lord Vishnu worship. "Dhanur" refers to Sagittarius (Dhanus) constellation. Special observances include:
- Daily Suprabhatam seva
- Special archanas and abhishekams
- Reading of Bhagavad Gita
- Fasting on specific days
- Increased devotional activities

During this month, devotees wake early for temple worship and special prayers.

VAIKUNTA EKADASI (Dec 20):
The most important Ekadasi of the year. Believed that the gates of Vaikunta (Lord Vishnu's heavenly abode) are open on this day. Those who worship on this day attain moksha (liberation). Also called Mukkoti Ekadasi or Swarga Vathil Ekadasi.

Special observances:
- Fasting (complete or partial)
- All-night vigil (singing bhajans, reading scriptures)
- Procession through "Vaikunta Dwaram" (Heaven's gate)
- Special poojas and decorations
- Reading of Vaikunta Gadyam

This Ekadasi falls during Dhanurmasam, making it doubly auspicious.

CHRISTMAS (Dec 25):
Christian festival celebrating birth of Jesus Christ.

KARTIKA SOMAVARA (Dec 7):
Last Monday of Kartika month with special Siva Abhishekam.
