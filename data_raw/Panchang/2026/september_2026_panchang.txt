SPECIAL SEPTEMBER FESTIVALS:

SRI KRISHNASHTAMI (Sep 4):
Birthday of Lord Krishna, eighth avatar of Vishnu. Celebrated with fasting, midnight poojas (birth time), devotional singing, and enactment of Krishna's life events. Dahi Handi celebrations.

SRI VINAYAKA CHATURTHI (Sep 14):
Ganesh Chaturthi - Birthday of Lord Ganesha. Ten-day festival with installation of Ganesha idols, daily poojas, and cultural programs.

SRI VINAYAKA NIMAJJANAM (Sep 19):
Ganesh Visarjan - Immersion of Ganesha idols marking the end of Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations.

SRI VENKATESWARA SWAMY JAYANTI (Sep 22):
Birthday of Lord Venkateswara. Special celebrations at all Venkateswara temples.

SRI VEDANTA DESIKAR TIRUNAKSHATRAM (Sep 22):
Birth star of Sri Vedanta Desika, great Vaishnava philosopher and poet (1268-1369).

PAVITROTSAVAM (Sep 24-27):
Important temple festival for purification and renewal. "Pavitra" means sacred thread.
- Ankurarpanam (Sep 24): Ceremonial planting
- Pavitra Samarpana (Sep 25): Offering of sacred threads
- Shanti Kalyanam (Sep 27): Divine wedding concluding the festival

MAHALAYA PAKSHAM (Starting Sep 27):
Fifteen-day period for ancestral rites (Pitru Paksha). Hindus perform Shraddha ceremonies for deceased ancestors. Ends with Mahalaya Amavasya (Oct 10).
