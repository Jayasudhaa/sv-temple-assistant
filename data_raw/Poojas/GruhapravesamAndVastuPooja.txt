GRUHAPRAVESAM (HOUSEWARMING) AND VASTU POOJA

CEREMONY INFORMATION:
Service Name: Gruhapravesam with Vastu Pooja
Also Known As: Housewarming Ceremony, Griha Pravesh
Deity: Lord Ganesha, Vastu Purusha, Family Deities
Category: Life Ceremony / Auspicious Home Ritual
Location: Performed at devotee's new home

PURPOSE:
Gruhapravesam is the traditional Hindu housewarming ceremony performed when entering a new home for the first time. Vastu Pooja is performed to invoke blessings of Vastu Purusha (deity of directions and space) for peace, prosperity, and positive energy in the new home.

WHEN TO PERFORM:
- When moving into a newly constructed house
- When moving into a new home (resale/rental)
- On an auspicious day as per panchang (consult temple)
- Typically includes: Vastu Shanti, Gruhapravesam, optional Satyanarayana Vratham
