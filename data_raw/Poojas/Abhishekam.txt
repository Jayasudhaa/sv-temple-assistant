WHAT IS ABHISHEKAM?

Abhishekam is a sacred Hindu ritual of bathing deity idols with holy substances like milk, honey, yogurt, ghee, and other sacred items while chanting mantras.

PURPOSE:
- Worship and honor the deity
- Seek blessings and divine grace
- Purification and spiritual benefits
