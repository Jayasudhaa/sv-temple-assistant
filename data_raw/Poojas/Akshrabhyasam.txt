WHAT IS AKSHARAABHYASAM?

Child's first writing ceremony marking the beginning of education. The child writes their first letters on this auspicious day.

WHEN: Age 3-5 years (Saraswati Puja day most auspicious)
DEITIES: Goddess Saraswati (knowledge), Lord Ganesha (removing obstacles)
