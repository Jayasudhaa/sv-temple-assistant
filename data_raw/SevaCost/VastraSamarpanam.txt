VASTRA SAMARPANAM

Vastra Samarpana is a devotional offering of clothes in rituals, symbolizes respect and devotion towards the God/Goddess, embodying surrender and the devotee's desire to clothe the divine with love and reverence.

Vastras at our temple are typically offered on first Saturday of the month (Abhishekam) to Venkateswara Swamy, second Saturday of the month (Kalyanam) to Malayappa swamy, Sridevi and Bhudevi, third Friday of the month (Abhishekam) to Andal Ammavaru and third Saturday of the month (Abhishekam) to Mahalakshmi Ammavaru.

You can either bring your own Vastram or buy from Temple. Sarees will be returned to the devotee upon request the following month.
