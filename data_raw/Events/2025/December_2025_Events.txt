SIGNIFICANCE:
Karthika Deepam is a festival of lights dedicated to Lord Shiva. It falls on the full moon day in the Tamil month of Karthikai (November-December). The festival celebrates the manifestation of Lord Shiva as an infinite column of light (Jyoti).

CELEBRATIONS:
- Lighting of lamps (deepas) throughout the temple
- Special abhishekam to Lord Shiva
- Deepa aradhana (lamp offering ceremony)
- Community gathering and prayers
